Overview

Alicorp is an industry leader in the food sector in Peru, with presence across multiple countries in the Americas, sales of $1.79 billion, and more than 4,000 employees, a member of Grupo Romero.

I led research projects to completion across processes, products, health, and nutrition, providing support for Technological Development and Regulatory Affairs and reporting to the Research and Regulatory Affairs Manager.

Over three years, I introduced data automation and software development into a research function traditionally driven by experimental lab work, building Python desktop and Django web applications that transformed how the team worked with nutritional data and identified cost reduction opportunities with projected annual savings of ~$133K.

Key contributions

  • Python | Statistical analysis: Spearheaded nutritional analytics workflows in Python, introducing comparative graphical indices (SAIN-LIM) to assess and benchmark performance, using Pandas, Tkinter, Matplotlib, NumPy, and PyInstaller for a standalone desktop application with projected potential savings of up to $50K over two years.
  • Django web application: Built and deployed a Django-based web application on Microsoft Azure, enabling distributed team members to access centralized nutritional databases and generate standardized reports.
  • Data visualization | Data storytelling: Produced executive-level visualizations using Matplotlib and Seaborn that translated analytical findings into actionable business and regulatory recommendations.
  • AI tools and innovation: Provided AI tools training to the Technological Development team; led an initiative to streamline sensory team workflows by automating test data analysis and implementing AI tools to expedite profile evaluations.
  • Research publications: Published one article in the World Journal of Food Science and Technology (WJFST) and two articles in Industria Alimentaria; received award recognition from Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú for two collaborative research projects.
  • Regulatory expertise: Applied working knowledge of Peruvian food labeling regulations (LAS-1, LAS-2), FDA, and EFSA frameworks to support compliance review across Alicorp's product categories in multiple markets.
  • Project leadership | Cross-functional collaboration: Led the full research portfolio during manager absences and resolved critical cross-functional issues, including international vendor escalations; worked across Innovation, Product Development, Quality, Process Engineering, Marketing, and Data Analysis, bridging chemical engineering domain knowledge with computational methods.

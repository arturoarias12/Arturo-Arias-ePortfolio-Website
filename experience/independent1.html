Overview

Incarail is Peru's premier luxury passenger rail company, operating iconic routes through the Andes, including the journey to Machu Picchu.

As a Business Intelligence Specialist Consultant engaged to support Incarail's strategic digital initiatives, I was responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining BI dashboards, generating key performance indicators, and automating executive reports; providing technical advisory and training to internal teams, proposing data-driven solutions to enhance decision-making within a Scrum-based environment.

Key contributions

  • Data visualization | Business intelligence: Engineered Power BI dashboards on Microsoft Fabric, delivering e-commerce sales and web performance insights, applying industry best practices to ensure performance, usability, and stakeholder value.
  • Performance optimization: Streamlined Microsoft Fabric dashboards, reducing file size by 92% and refresh processing time by 90%, improving user experience and lowering Microsoft Fabric capacity costs.
  • ETL/ELT | Data pipelines: Devised and refined Fabric data pipelines that centralized sources, streamlined transformations, and ensured reliable data availability for reporting and monitoring.
  • Python | PySpark | Data processing: Automated OCR workflows for PDFs in Fabric using PySpark, eliminating manual effort and accelerating reporting cycles.
  • SQL | Query optimization: Optimized BigQuery queries and partitions on Google Cloud Platform, reducing execution time by 80%+ and lowering compute spend.
  • Technical advisory: Provided BI support to the e-commerce, revenue, and UX teams at Incarail, delivering high-quality dashboards aligned with ongoing digital and commercial strategies within a Scrum-based team.

01 - About

About me

I am Arturo, a Data and AI professional currently completing a Master of Information Systems Management - Business Intelligence and Data Analytics at Carnegie Mellon University. My work focuses on transforming complex data into practical systems, from BI dashboards and analytics pipelines to automation tools and trained AI models, that reduce friction, improve decisions, and give people back time.

My path began in chemical engineering, where I learned to approach problems with structure, curiosity, and systems thinking. Today, I apply that foundation across data analytics, business intelligence, machine learning, and AI-powered automation. Certified in Python programming and AI product design, I also mentor early-career data professionals and work to open pathways for others pursuing growth through technology.

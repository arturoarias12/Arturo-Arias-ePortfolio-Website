Things I care about

Causes

Efforts that can compound across communities and generations.

Education

Knowledge is one of the few things that multiplies when shared. Teaching, mentoring, student chapters, and open knowledge initiatives create opportunities that extend far beyond a single person, strengthening communities through learning and collaboration.

Financial literacy

Understanding money, markets, and long-term financial planning is a skill most people are never formally taught. Closing that gap matters, especially for first-generation professionals navigating these decisions without a roadmap.

AI for everyone

AI has the potential to amplify human capability across education, science, business, and everyday life. Inclusive tools, accessible learning resources, and clear conversations about the strengths and limits of these systems can help broaden participation and create more meaningful impact across communities.

Cancer support

Healthcare can have a profound impact on people and families during vulnerable moments. Supporting access to care, treatment, and community resources is one way to help reduce uncertainty and improve quality of life for those who need it most.
